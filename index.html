About Me

I am a software engineer at Google New York, working on making Google better answer questions. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from NLP group of University of Pennsylvania, advised by Mitch Marcus and Ani Nenkova. My thesis focuses on content selection in multi-document summarization. I did my undergraduate study in Tsinghua University, where I was a member of Yao Class.
